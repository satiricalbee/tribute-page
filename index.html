JPEGMAFIA

Here's a time line of Barrington DeVaughn Hendricks life:

  • 1989 Barrington DeVaughn Hendricks is born in Flatbush, New York
  • 2002 DeVaughn would move to Alabama this year, where he would experienced a lot of racism, which later had an intense effect on his music
  • 2007 Hendricks enlists for the Air Force, then serves in Iraq, Kuwait, Germany, Japan, and North Africa
  • 2009 Hendricks would release his first album: "DREAMCAST SUMMER SONGS"
  • 2015 "Peggy" would be honorably discharged from the Air Force and then would move to Baltimore to work under the moniker "JPEGMAFIA" releasing two mixtapes and an album
  • 2018 JPEGMAFIA would release his second, critically acclaimed, album. This would put him further in the public eye, furthering his audience
  • 2019-2022 Hendricks would go on to release 3 more projects in this time, each one being more refined than the previous
